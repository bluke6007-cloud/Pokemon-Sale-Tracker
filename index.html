Google Sheets — sync to YOUR card sheet
This connects to the same sheet you already use for your card list — the one with Cost and Price columns. It won't create a separate sales sheet: when you log a sale, this tool finds the matching card row and fills in Buyer, Phone, Sold Price, Qty and Date Sold right there. Your Cost column stays in the sheet only — it's never sent to buyers.

One-time setup (5 min):
1. Open your existing card spreadsheet (or sheets.new to make one). Make sure the sheet has header names Card, Cost, Price (and optionally Set) in row 1
2. Go to Extensions → Apps Script → paste the script below → click Save & Deploy as Web App (Execute as: Me, Who has access: Anyone)
3. Copy the Web App URL and paste it below
